About Us

We are three Kellogg students looking to revolutionize the way
fans interact with their stars.

Sports Knowledge

Sports is one of the most popular and generally understood
areas of personal and emotional investment. Fans spend countless
hours watching and evaluating their favorite athletes and teams,
and going as far as to find mediums for financial and competitive
gain as sports viewers via outlets including fantasy sports
leagues, memorabilia collection, and the growing space of
legalized sports gambling.

Connect

While there are are currently external
offerings that allow fans to preliminarily tie their financial
gain to their preexisting sports fandom, they don’t give fans an
inherent connection to said athletes that they idealize.

Invest

But what if there was a way that the average person could invest in
their favorite sports personalities, providing the idyllic
“team owner” feeling to the average fan, just as fantasy sports
have empowered every fan to feel like a coach or manager?

"Team Owner"

We will merge the passion and emotional investment of sports fans
with the enticing and emerging worlds of democratized investments
and cryptocurrency, giving fans a small taste of the “team owner” experience.

Earn Unique Perks

Not only can fans have a sense of pride in being part of their
favorite athletes journey, but owning the coin will also
let them earn "medals of honor" for their loyalty, giving
them exclusive fan experiences as recognition for their investment
and dedication.
